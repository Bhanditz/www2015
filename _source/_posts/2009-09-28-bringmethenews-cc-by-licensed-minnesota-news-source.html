BringMeTheNews is a new news source for Minnesotans looking for a single destination to both aggregate and produce interesting and relevant stories. Developed by Rick Kupchella, BringMeTheNews is attempting to leverage the power of social media (specifically Facebook and Twitter), Kupchella's reputation as a journalist, a unique approach to advertising, and Creative Commons licensing
