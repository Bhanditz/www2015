Vocoid is a local San Francisco net label/collective dedicated to supporting musicians by providing space and shared promotion. Last week they announced the first ever Vocoid Remix Contest. The contest utilizes ccMixter as the platform and the raw audio from their album Medly for your mixing and mashing pleasure. Check out their website for contest rules, information and other cool events.

According to co-founder Doug Land, Vocoid has been a longtime supporter of Creative Commons and ccMixter. They produced the Creatively Uncommon 2006 compilation in celebration of CC's 4th Birthday.
