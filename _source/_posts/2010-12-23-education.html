Open Educational Resources
Creative Commons licenses provide a globally-recognized framework for open educational resources (OER) & learning materials that are freely available to use, remix, and redistribute. CC licenses enable teachers, learners, and educational institutions to share the legal rights to use, remix, and redistribute textbooks, lesson plans, video lectures, and more.

OER are a response to the barriers in education that currently exist. How often are the resources you need behind a paywall or under "all rights reserved" copyright? Do you ever wish a resource was available in another language or format, or to improve outdated or incorrect information? Have you ever tried combining resources together to create a new resource - only to find that you can't because the different resources don't interoperate (work together legally)? How much time do you spend finding the resources you actually need?

CC licenses and other legal tools address and largely overcome the legal and technical restrictions that prevent educational resources from being accessible, adaptable, interoperable, and discoverable.
