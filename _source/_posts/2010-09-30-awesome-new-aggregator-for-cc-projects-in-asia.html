Creative Commons jurisdiction projects in Asia now have a gorgeous, new landing page. The site aggregates blog feeds and photostreams from twelve CC teams across the Asian continent. You can find the CC latest news from the region and contact information so that you, too, can get involved.

The domain creativecommons.asia is made possible thanks to the generous support of DotAsia, and excellent design work was led by the Fabricatorz team. Thanks, everyone!
