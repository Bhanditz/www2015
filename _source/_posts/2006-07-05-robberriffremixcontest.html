Monkey Love Records and Fake Science are sponsoring a super cool CC-licensed remix contest for the Robberriff track "Stringing Away Revisited." You have until July 16 to "rinse, cut, splice, add samples and do whatever the hell you want to it." Fake Science will choose the top 20 remixes and post them online for people to rate. The producer of the highest-ranked track will win an iPod nano. Join the fun!
