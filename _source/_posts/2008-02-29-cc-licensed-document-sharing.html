For those of you looking to share documents, templates, and other digital documentation online, make sure to check out both Scribd and DocStoc. Both sites have CC license implementation built in and make sharing your documents with co-workers, friends, family, and the general public a breeze. There is an immense amount of options available for both Scribd and Docstoc (read their respective FAQs here and here) and while they are both fairly flexible, Scribd is more focused on personal documents (creative, individual, etc.) while Docstoc is oriented towards a professional (legal, business, technology, etc.) audience.
