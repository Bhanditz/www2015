From a "Happy Birthday" rendition in Korean to apple schnapps in Reykjavik and a Free Culture debate in Poland, Creative Commons' seventh birthday was celebrated this month with originality and cheerful camaraderie.

The global parties kicked off in Beijing at the opening of "Remix and Share", a contemporary art exhibition featuring 60 acclaimed artists across the country. Afterward a discussion forum explored the event's theme, led by CC Project Leads and representatives from Taiwan, Hong Kong, Australia, and others in the Asia-Pacific region.
