I was not expecting our humble NYC CC Salon / Birthday to become so popular that I had to find a new venue, but that's what has happened.

With over 140 people and 76 maybes RSVP'd, TOPP and I were a little worried about whether the building would still be standing after the event.

So our friends at For Your Imagination have graciously offered us their loft for the event on Tuesday. I've amended the address on the Facebook event and blog post, but here it is anyway:


Tuesday, December 16th from 7-10pm
For Your Imagination
22 W. 27th St., 6th Floor
New York, NY

As usual there will be beer and a reception afterwards, so we look forward to seeing you!
