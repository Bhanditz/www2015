One of my favorite parts of my job as Creative Commons' Culture Program Associate in New York is that I get to organize our Salons here. With that in mind, it gives me great pleasure to announce the next CC Salon NYC.

The Open Planning Project's has graciously allowed us to use their wonderful loft space in the West Village. July's salon will feature presentations from Search Wikia, Livable Streets Network, and a special performance from comedian Max Silvestri (of Gabe + Max's Internet Thing).

Here are the details:

CC Salon NYC
July 23rd, 7-10pm
The Open Planning Project, 349 W. 12th St.

We are also happy to announce that we'll be supplying free beer (free as in beer, that is) for a reception afterwards. So come for the presentations, stay for the drinks and
