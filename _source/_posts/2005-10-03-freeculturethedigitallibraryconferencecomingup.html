Just as digitizing libraries has become a very topical issue, the MetaScholar Initiative is holding a Free Culture & the Digital Library conference at Emory University in Atlanta, Georgia, US, on October 14, 2005. CC's own CEO & Chairman, Lawrence Lessig and New York University Assistent Professor of Culture and Communication Siva Vaidhyanathan will be among the panel of experts discussing "the relationship between digital access to public cultural information and intellectual property constraints."
