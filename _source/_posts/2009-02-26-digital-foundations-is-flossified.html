Michael Mandiberg and Adam Hyde teamed up a couple of weekends ago at the non-profit digital artspace Eyebeam in New York City to translate Michael's CC licensed book, Digital Foundations, into FLOSS software instructions. While the original version of Digital Foundations requires access to Adobe's proprietary CS suite to follow the instructions and screen shots, the new FLOSSmanuals.net version teaches you the fundamentals of design using free-as-in-speech software.

Around 25 volunteers convened at Eyebeam over the course of 3 days to translate Digital Foundations from Adobe to FLOSS (Free Libre Open Sources Software) applications, making good on the promise of Digital Foundationsâ€™ Creative Commons license.
