Artists from different musical genres (hip-hop, rap, jazz, folk music) met in Tunis in July, 2011 to celebrate the creativity and innovation brought by Arab youth to the Arab revolutions which erupted in Tunisia in December, 2010 and spread across the region. The project was hosted as part of the third CC Arab regional meeting in cooperation with Tunisian blogger platform Nawaat and Al Jazeera Networks. An artists` residence lasted three days, and musicians were encouraged to create original music collectively, each infusing the project with his/her own musical genre. More artists have added their voices and sounds, and the result is now available to be shared and remixed freely under CC BY-NC.

Remix competition

"It Will Be Wonderful" is available for everyone all over the world to download and build on it. We will publish the best remixes in a CC-licensed CD/book/DVD. We also hope to have a party in an Arab capital to celebrate the project where the best remixers will be invited to join.

Some of the members of the "It Will Be Wonderful" music collective will be performing in Rome on April 16th in the stunning location of Ara Pacis museum. We look forward to organizing more live shows and invite the best remixers to join us on a global "It Will Be Wonderful" tour!
