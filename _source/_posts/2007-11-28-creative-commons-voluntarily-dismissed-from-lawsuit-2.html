We are happy to report that Creative Commons has been voluntarily dismissed by the plaintiffs from a lawsuit against Virgin Mobile.

Please see our press release and a blog entry with more information, including a copy of the dismissal, from our CEO, Lawrence Lessig.

We would like to especially thank our many legal friends for pro-bono counsel.
