There is an incredibly comprehensive and informative profile on John Buckman, founder of Magnatune, at the Open Rights Group wiki. The profile contains an immense amount of information, outlining the motivation behind Magnatune, Buckman's thoughts on the future of the music industry, and a bevy of info regarding Magnatune and CC. Consider it the most in depth Featured Commoner piece ever committed to HTML, and we didn't even create it.
