Ten confirmed global CC@5 parties in eight countries spanning six time zones: how to catch it all?

The generous team at n3tv.it, alongside Berlin's seasoned Erik Stripparo, have offered to help. Starting in Berlin December 14th at 2000 UTC (2100 Central European Time), we will be streaming CC Birthday Parties around the globe.

You can view the action live by visiting www.n3tv.it or www.mogulus.com/n3tv, or DIY by embedding the player in your own blog or website by copy/pasting the following script:

<script src=" http://www.mogulus.com/scripts/playerchromeless.js?channel=n3tv&bgcolor=0x000000&showviewers=true&width=454&height=389&on=true " type="text/javascript"></script>

And remember: if you're joining a birthday bash in your area, don't forget to use the tag "CCBday5"!
