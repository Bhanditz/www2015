We started CC Salon in San Francisco less than a year ago and have watched excitedly as the idea has spread internationally. Salons are now being held in Warsaw, Seoul, Beijing, Johannesburg, Brisbane, Berlin (called "Creative Commons Usergroup"), NYC, London, Taipei, and now Amsterdam!

"The first Dutch ccSalon will kick off on Wed. Dec. 13th at 20:30 in 'De Melkweg' Amsterdam and will be focused on music and sounds."
