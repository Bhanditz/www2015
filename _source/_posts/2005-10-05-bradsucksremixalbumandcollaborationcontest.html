Brad Sucks is a staff favorite musician that posts all his music online and under CC licenses. Brad's posted most of his tracks to Mixter and got enough remixes in response that he recently collected them into a complete remix album (based on his original songs). Now he's part of a new Online Collaboration Contest over at MyVirtualBand. The terms for all contributions are CC licensed as well. We here at Creative Commons are looking forward to all the collaborative music that comes from musicians all around the world thanks to this contest.
