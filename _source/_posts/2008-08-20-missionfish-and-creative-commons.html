MissionFish has partnered with Ebay to help nonprofits receive funds through the online auction community. Now that Creative Commons has established ourselves with MissionFish, our supporters can

in three different ways:
* Ebay sellers can designate that a portion or all of their profit will go to Creative Commons; 2) CC can sell items directly on Ebay (such as in-kind donations we can't use), and our seller profile will specify that we are a non-profit and that the revenue goes toward our org; 3) Ebayers can donate to CC through Paypal.

If you want to read more about any of these three modes of fundraising, go to http://www.missionfish.org/fundraise/us_fundraise.html. It's all laid out in a concise and easy to understand manner.
