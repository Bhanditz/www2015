PublicSpacesLab, a CC-using netlabel, recently launched "Music for Freedom", a project that encourages musicians (and non-musicians) to engage with the Iranian election through online collaboration. Co-sponsored by CC-content directories Soundcloud and Indaba Music, the "Music For Freedom" project is aggregating samples of the word 'freedom' spoken by their community in a variety of languages. These samples are then then imported to a collaborative Indaba session where a community of musicians is collaborating on a singular piece.

The final mixdown will be released under a CC BY-SA license - you can join the conversation here.
