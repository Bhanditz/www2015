Positron! Records is now offering their artists the option of releasing material under the Sampling or Sampling+ licenses.

Christopher Randall of Positron says: "...our first two actual releases under the CC Sampling+ license are right around the corner. The first record, called 'in a haze' by the noise artist s.sturgis, will be released on Nov. 23rd, and is at the pressing plant as I type this.

We will be changing the licenses of all our releases to Sampling or Sampling+ as each one gets repressed, unless the artist in question has strictly forbidden it (and we do have one that won't toe the line)."

A very lengthy, well worth reading, can be found on Chris' blog.

The label's music styles are wide ranging and a very welcome addition to Commons sample pool.
