If you haven't already, check out Scripta, CC Latin America's new publication available online (CC BY). The editorial committee for Scripta comes from all over Latin America with contributors from Chile, Colombia, Guatemala, and Ecuador. One of our own, intern Grace Armstrong, partakes in this issue with an in depth Q&A introduction to ccLearn and open education. Even if you don't speak Spanish, the graphics alone---that brilliant turtle on the cover---is worth clicking to.
