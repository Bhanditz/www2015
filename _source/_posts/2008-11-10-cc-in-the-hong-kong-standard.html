There is a great write-up on CC in a recent edition of the Hong Kong Standard - the article coincided with the launch of CC Hong Kong, our fiftieth jurisdiction to enact license porting. Focusing on the wonderful anecdote of former Featured Commoner Colin Mutchler, how CC licenses work, and how CC is increasing collaborative efforts for Hong Kong residents, the article is a simple and clear introduction to CC for a new geographical locale.
