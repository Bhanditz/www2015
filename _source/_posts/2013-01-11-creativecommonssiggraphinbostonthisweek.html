Creative Commons is represented this week as part of the first ever Open Source Pavilion at the world's largest Graphic Conference, Siggraph. The booth this year is set-up by Ton Rosendaal, the project lead of the CC-BY 2.5 licensed Elephant's Dream Open Source movie.

Please stop by booth #1527 all week to discuss Creative Commons, licensing, and how you can plug into the Creative Commons Developer Community. Also, click here to get a free pass to get into the exhibition. It will save you some cash.

This conference participation comes on the heals of the previous week's successful Desktop Developer Conference in Ottawa, Canada, where Jon Phillips (myself) presented "May I See Your License?" which looked at the technical side of Creative Commons and similar aforementioned topics.

Look for Creative Commons upcoming participation in Linux World August 14-17 in San Francisco at the Moscone Center.
