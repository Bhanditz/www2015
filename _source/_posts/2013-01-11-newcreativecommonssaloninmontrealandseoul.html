Looks like Evan Prodroumou, of wikitravel fame, participated in another Canadian CC Salon in Montreal.

Also, Creative Commons Korea is launching their ongoing CC Salon series with a look into the "MUSICCOMMONS" on August 19 in Seoul's excellent Apgujeong area. Please attend if you are in or traveling to Korea during that time.

If you are interested in starting a Creative Commons Salon in your city, please do so and help us spread cc. First though, please check out the Creative Commons Salon in San Francisco tonite!
