A view of one of the UNESCO buildings that will be officially open to the public on 3 November, - the Secretariat -- a Y-shaped, seven-storey building raised on "pilotis" and walled with glass. There are two other main units -- a conference building with fluted concrete sides and a "butterfly" roof; and a smaller, square four-storey building to house permanent delegations to UNESCO and nongovernmental organizations. 1/Sep/1958.
