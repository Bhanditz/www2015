Copy Me/Remix Me is the second CD assembled and released by Creative Commons. The following is a list of all the submitted remixes of BM Relocation Program's Superego Exchange song (original song here), which we held a mini-contest for. We picked out our favorites for the album, but you're welcome to download all the entires and listen to them as well.

Note: All songs listed below are released under the identical Attribution-ShareAlike license. We'd like to thank BM Relocation Program for participating in it and providing all the parts of their song, and we'd also like to thank Opsound for hosting BM Relocation Program's music as well as dozens of other bands and artists.
