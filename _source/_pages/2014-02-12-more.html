Wiki

Our public wiki is a place to develop a collective memory about Creative Commons, contribute to our on-going projects, and a great place to find ample amounts of ways to help grow the commons.

Live chat

You can join Creative Commons staff and friends on IRC in #creativecommons on irc.freenode.net. You can join this channel in your browser too.

Additionally, the CC developer community uses the IRC chat channel #creativecommons-dev on irc.freenode.net. Please note, this channel is primarily used for free software-style development discussions, so it might be a bit technical.
