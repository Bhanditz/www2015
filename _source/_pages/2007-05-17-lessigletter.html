Creative Commons' Lawrence Lessig occasionally sends an email newsletter with updates on our activities. To subscribe to or unsubscribe from the newsletter, see below.

If you use a RSS (feed) reader, you may wish to subscribe to our weblog's RSS feed for more frequent updates.

Subscribe

To subscribe, enter your email address below:


You will receive a confirmation email, which you will need to respond to in order to complete your subscription (for protection against unwanted singups).

Unsubscribe

If you are a subscriber, you have two unsubscription options:

  • Send an email to cc-lessigletter-request@lists.ibiblio.org with "unsubscribe" in the subject line (clicking on the link should open an email with To: and Subject: prefilled for you--just send the email without writing anything else).
  • Alternatively, visit http://lists.ibiblio.org/mailman/options/cc-lessigletter, enter your email address (ignore the password field), and click "unsubscribe." You will receive a confirmation email.
