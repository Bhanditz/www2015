Newsletter

The CC Newsletter goes out every other month and is an excellent resource for all members of our community, new and longstanding, who care about CC and want to stay up to date with current CC happenings across the globe.

Since we switched over our newsletters to a web-friendly format, the current digital archive of CC newsletters is available at creativecommons.org/newsletter. We also keep an archive of the beautifully-designed PDF versions thanks to the CC Philippines team.

Subscribe to our newsletter.

Wiki

Our public wiki is a place to develop a collective memory about Creative Commons, contribute to our on-going projects, and a great place to find ample amounts of ways to help grow the commons.

Chat

The CC developer community uses the IRC chat channel #cc on irc.freenode.net. If that doesn't make sense to you, you can read more here about hopping onto this chat channel to find out how to contribute. Please note, this channel is primarily used for free software-style development discussions, so it might be a bit technical.

Web Forum

A forum of topics related to Creative Commons, including general questions about CC licenses. It is a good place to start if you are interested in Creative Commons and want to ask general questions.
